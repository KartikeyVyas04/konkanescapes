Sinhagad Fort

Lion's Fort - Pune's Iconic Trek

History & Legend

Perched atop the Sahyadri mountains, Sinhagad (formerly Kondhana) was pivotal in Maratha history. In 1670, Tanaji Malusare scaled sheer cliffs using a lizard (Ghorpad) to recapture it from Mughals in a daring night raid. Shivaji mourned, saying "Gad aala, pan sinh gela" ‚Äì The fort is won, but the lion is lost. This 760m high fort offers views of Rajgad and Purandar.[web:4][page:1][web:9]
